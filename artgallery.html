The Scream

Edward Munch

This popular painting has attracted many people, amazed even more, and scared a few. It became one of the most iconic painting picture, seen as symbolising the anxiety of the human condition.

Girl with a Pearl Earing

Edward Munch

In 2006, the Dutch public selected it as the most beautiful painting in the Netherlands. It depicts a European girl wearing an exotic dress, an oriental turban, and an improbably large pearl earring.

The Arnolfini Portrait

Jan Van Eyck

One of the most significant works produced during the Northern Renaissance, this composition is believed to be one of the first paintings executed in oils.
